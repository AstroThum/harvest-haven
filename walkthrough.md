# Harvest Haven v3.0 Walkthrough

The **Harvest Haven v3.0 Survival Roguelike Expansion** has been successfully implemented! 

The game has been transformed from a casual farming simulator into an intense survival roguelike with a town, a cave, a fishing pond, and an epic wave-based defense mechanic!

## What's New

### 🏠 Map Expansion & Architecture
The map now features interactive zones you can walk to. The previous `Shop` building on your farm has been moved to the new `TownScene`. Walk to the edges of the Farm to discover the new areas:
*   **North**: Town Square (Shop, NPCs)
*   **East**: Fishing Pond
*   **South**: Mining Cave

### 🧟‍♂️ Nighttime Defense Mechanics
Instead of just random enemies wandering into your farm at night, there is now an organized **Wave Defense event**!
At 75% of the day's progression, a `DefenseScene` will trigger:
- Enemies will spawn in fast waves.
- You must defeat all enemies in the wave to survive the night.
- If you survive, you'll be heavily rewarded with gold and return to the farm for the night.
- **Nightmare Mode**: Enemies also spawn constantly during the day on top of the night wave!

### 🗣️ The Town & NPCs
Head North from the farm to visit the Town!
- **The General Store** now lives here. It offers Seeds, Weapons, Sprinklers, and the highly anticipated **House Upgrades**.
- **NPCs** (Mayor, Blacksmith, Merchant, Angler, Explorer) wander the town. Press `E` to talk to them, and gift them your harvested crops to build your Friendship level!

### 🎣 Fishing Minigame
Walk East from the farm to find the Pond!
- **Cast your line** and wait for a bite. 
- You'll engage in a **Stardew Valley-style timing minigame** where you must keep a green bar over the fish as it darts up and down. Catch the fish and it will be added to your inventory!

### ⛏️ The Mining Cave
Venture South from the farm into the procedurally generated Caves!
- Use your **Pickaxe** (swing your sword near the rocks) to break them open.
- Deeper levels have higher chances of dropping rare ores (`Stone`, `Copper`, `Iron`, `Gold`, `Diamond`).
- Finding a ladder lets you descend deeper. Watch out for the supercharged slimes that lurk below!

### 🏡 House Upgrades & Automation
Your home is now more important than ever:
- **Upgrades**: You can upgrade your house at the shop (Level 2: 2500g, Level 3: 10000g). Better houses restore more health while sleeping.
- **Sprinklers**: Buy sprinklers for 500g and place them on your farm. They will automatically water a cross-pattern of adjacent tiles overnight!

> [!TIP]
> **Pro Tip**: Use your early gold to buy at least one Sprinkler to save time in the morning, and gift some cheap crops to NPCs to build early Friendship points!

> [!WARNING]
> If your health reaches 0 at any point, the game is over and your survival streak will be recorded to the High Score!
